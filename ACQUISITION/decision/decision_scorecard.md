# Decision Scorecard — Is Acquisition the Right 12-Month Goal?

Use this to score **Path C (owner-operator acquisition)** against your baseline paths and against your profile. Re-score when you have real listing data or financing numbers.

---

## Weighted criteria (default)

| Criterion | Weight | What it measures |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| `fit_with_strengths` | 25% | Messy workflow, pricing/ops leverage, product-led improvement, small-team operator |
| `financing_feasibility` | 25% | Equity + debt + runway; can close in 12 months without catastrophic personal risk |
| `expected_12m_outcome` | 20% | Defensible number, income, credibility — vs Paths A/B |
| `risk_of_relapse` | 20% | Novelty trap, domain drift, avoidance of current lane reps |
| `operating_enjoyment` | 10% | Would you run this for 3 years through boring middle |

**Bias:** Cash-flow reality and operator fit over prestige. Compounding simplicity over optionality.

---

## Scoring scale (1–5 per criterion)

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Strong yes — evidence from your track record supports this |
| 4 | Likely yes — minor gaps |
| 3 | Unclear — needs specific deal or personal finance data |
| 2 | Likely no — conflicts with REPENTANCE constraints |
| 1 | Hard no — relapse or credibility trap |

---

## Proceed / hold / kill thresholds

| Weighted total | Call |
|----------------|------|
| ≥ 3.5 | `Yes, but narrow` — pursue only archetypes that score 4+ on fit |
| 2.5 – 3.4 | `Not yet` — parallel 30-day reps test; don't pause Paths A/B |
| < 2.5 | `No` — acquisition is distraction this year |

**Current default call: `Not yet`** (2.35) until financing and a specific archetype raise fit/feasibility.

## Per-deal override template

When you have a real opportunity, copy this block:

```markdown
### Deal: [name]
- Archetype: 
- Asking / SDE: 
- Fit with strengths (1-5): 
- Financing feasible (1-5): 
- 12m outcome (1-5): 
- Relapse risk (1-5): 
- Operating enjoyment (1-5): 
- Weighted total: 
- Passes all three gates? (improve / buy / operate 3yr): Y/N
```

---

## Three gates (all required)

From the plan — only pursue if **all** pass:

1. **business_you_could_improve** — your skills (pricing, workflow, AI ops) are the value lever
2. **business_you_could_realistically_buy** — price, financing, seller transition feasible in 12 months
3. **business_you_would_want_to_operate_for_3_years** — not a 18-month flip fantasy

## Weekly Monday check (from `too-greedy.md`)

If considering acquisition alongside current lane, answer:

1. Did the **one number** (ARR or job-search pipeline) move this week?
2. Did I do **distribution** (outbound, demos, applications) or hide in acquisition research?
3. Does every hour on listings trace to a named gate above?

If acquisition hours rise while outbound falls, trigger **kill rule** in [`final_decision_memo.md`](final_decision_memo.md).
