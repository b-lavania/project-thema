## Why not "No" permanently

You have **real** buy-side-adjacent strengths: BVXpress deal SaaS, PMI/HRIS integration, pricing architecture, Moovez quote-to-dispatch ops, workflow automation. A **narrow** acquisition (micro-SaaS in logistics/field-service) could compound your lane — but only with capital, seller pipeline, and **replacement** of parallel paths, not addition to them.

---

## Why not "Yes" now

1. **12-month EV** — Search + close + transition rarely beats a Series-A ops-AI hire on income and credibility in year one.
2. **One-bet rule** — Acquisition as a third track violates `too-greedy.md` unless Path A is explicitly retired.
3. **CTO prerequisite** — Unresolved Moovez blocker means Path A is unresolved; adding Path C spreads attention.
4. **Relapse risk** — Diligence memos reward insight; founder outbound rewards compounding.
5. **Financing gap** — No documented equity, SBA path, or runway in your materials.

## Kill rules (stop considering acquisition this year)

Trigger any → pause all acquisition search until January 2027 review:

1. Acquisition research **> 8 hrs/week** for 2 consecutive weeks while Path B outbound **< 5 hrs/week**
2. Month-5 pattern: "more interesting" listing in a **new domain** outside ops-AI/logistics
3. LOI or offer without financing pre-approval and 12-month runway model
4. Any deal failing **all three gates** (improve / buy / operate 3yr) in `decision_scorecard.md`
5. Path B offer accepted — acquisition pauses 6 months minimum
6. Quotely hits **5+ paying operators** — re-evaluate whether acquisition dilutes Path A
