## Notion PM templates

Jarrell's thread assumes collaborative tooling. If your team lives in Notion, start from Notion's built-in PM gallery and map to this repo:

| Notion template | PMOS equivalent |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Product Wiki | `context/<company>/` + `products/<name>/brief.md` |
| Meeting Notes | `workspace/meeting-notes.md` |
| Documentation | `templates/03-prd.md` + `products/<name>/` |
| User Research | `workspace/user-research.md` + `templates/08-jtbd.md` |
| Roadmap | `templates/02-roadmap.md` + `products/<name>/roadmap.md` |

**Workflow:** Draft in markdown here (portable, Cursor-friendly) → paste or sync to Notion/Figma/Miro for stakeholder review.
