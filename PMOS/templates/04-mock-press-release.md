# Mock press release (Working Backwards)

**PMOS bucket:** Strategy · Discovery  
**Collaborate in:** Google Docs · Notion · Markdown

> Amazon culture: write the **customer-facing announcement** before you build. Philosophy > rigid template. If the release isn't compelling, rethink the bet.

**Product / feature:**
**Author:**
**Target launch date (fictional):**

## Headline

(One line — customer benefit, not feature name.)

## Subheadline

Who it's for and the outcome in plain language.

## Opening paragraph

City, date — In the first sentence: who is the customer and what problem is solved?

## The problem (customer words)

What pain existed before this existed?

## The solution

How does the product work from the customer's perspective? No internal jargon.

## Customer quote

> "..." — Name, Title, Company (real or representative persona)

## Leader quote (optional)

> "..." — Why this matters to the company mission.

## How to get started

Pricing, signup, availability.
