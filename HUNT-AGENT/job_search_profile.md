# Job Search Profile

## Target roles

Core:
- Product Manager (IC — no direct reports)
- Technical Product Manager
- Technical Program Manager (TPM) — selectively
- Platform PM
- Chief of Staff / BizOps — selectively

**Exclude titles containing:** senior, sr, lead, staff, principal, head of, director, vp, chief (when paired with product/tech), group product, vice president.

## Locations

- Calgary, AB
- San Francisco, CA
- Remote (US and Canada)
- Onsite/hybrid anywhere in the US or Canada (willing to relocate)

## Industries & companies

- AI/ML product companies
- Logistics / Marketplace platforms
- HR tech / HRIS
- B2B SaaS
- Pricing / monetization
- Any size/stage (startup to enterprise)

**Exclude:** gambling, crypto, defense.

## Constraints

- Full-time IC role (no direct reports is strongly preferred)
- Remote-first preferred; open to SF or other US/CA hubs for hybrid
- No visa sponsorship needed (US/Canada work authorization)
- No return-to-office 5 days/week
