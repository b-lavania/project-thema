# Drill 4: Operator Translation

## Setup
- Pick a real product decision or problem
- Explain it three ways

## The Form

**Topic:** _________________________
**Date:** ___________

---

### Version 1: PM Language (3 min)
Speak like a product manager. Frameworks, users, journeys, hypotheses.

_________________________________________________

---

### Version 2: Executive / Commercial Language (3 min)
Speak like a CEO or CFO. Revenue, cost, risk, timeline, ROI.

_________________________________________________

---

### Version 3: Operator / Frontline Language (3 min)
Speak like the person doing the work. Actions, pain, workarounds, trust.

_________________________________________________

---

### The synthesis (1 min)
Which version is the truest? Which version would move the decision?

_________________________________________________

---

## Self-check

- [ ] All three versions completed
- [ ] Each version sounds natural to its audience
- [ ] No version is just the others with jargon swapped
- [ ] Synthesis identifies the leverage point
