# Drill 1: 15-Minute Product Teardown

## Setup
- Timer: 15 minutes, hard stop
- No research, no browsing, no "let me check"
- Use what you already know or can infer

## The Form

**Date:** ___________
**Time started:** _______  **Time finished:** _______
