# Scoring Rubric — PM Shock Cycle Bootcamp

## Points System

### Daily Quotas (60 points max per day)

| Quota | Points | Proof Required |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| Hard product decision written | 10 | One paragraph with recommendation + rationale |
| Written artifact completed | 10 | One page or less, signed off |
| Verbal communication rep | 10 | Recorded or transcribed, or witness confirmed |
| Backlog cut made | 10 | Item named, reason logged, cut confirmed |
| Anti-defensiveness rewrite | 10 | Before/after text saved |
| Scoreboard published | 10 | Timestamped entry shared with accountability partner |

**Daily score range: 0-60**

### Bonus Points (max 20 per day)

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Public artifact published (beyond weekly minimum) | +10 |
| Extra drill completed beyond quota | +5 |
| Simulated stakeholder conversation recorded | +5 |
| Executive compression drill completed under time | +5 |

### Penalties

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Miss 1 quota | -10, plus mandatory evening drill block |
| Miss 2+ quotas in one week | -20, plus mandatory weekend catch-up |
| Phone during first work block | -15 |
| Passive content before quotas hit | -10 |
| Hidden miss (not logged) | -30, grade automatically F |
| New side project started | -50, reset week |
| New identity experiment | -50, reset week |
| Skipped daily review | -20 |
| Skipped weekly audit | -40 |
| Missed weekly public artifact | -30, plus failure memo |

---

## Weekly Grade Thresholds

| Grade | Weekly Score Range | Meaning |
|-------|-------------------|---------|
| A | 400+ points, 0-1 miss, public artifact shipped | On track. Maintain. |
| B | 320-399, 1-2 misses corrected | Acceptable. Fix leaks. |
| C | 240-319, repeated misses or drift | Warning. Audit what's breaking. |
| F | <240 or any hidden miss/relapse | Failure. Write failure memo. Reset week. |

**Weekly score = sum of 7 daily scores + bonuses - penalties**

---

## Camp Success Threshold

To pass the 30-day shock cycle:

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Total camp score | 8,000+ points |
| Weeks graded A or B | 3 out of 4 |
| Zero F weeks | Required |
| Public artifacts shipped | 4 |
| Portfolio-grade strategy asset | 1 |
| Daily review streak | 25+ days |

---

## Camp Failure Conditions

- Any week graded F due to hidden miss or identity relapse
- Two consecutive C weeks
- Public artifact missed in two separate weeks
- New side project or identity started at any point

If you fail, you restart the week. No negotiation.
