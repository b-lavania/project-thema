# REPENTANCE — Positioning, Diagnosis, and Commitment

Three documents that form one arc: **outward** (what you sell) → **inward** (why you cap yourself) → **forward** (what to do about it).

## The theme

- *too-deep* — you buried your value under projects.
- *fatalism* — you believe the cap is who you are.
- *too-greedy* — the cure is wanting one thing narrowly enough to hold it.

Each file cross-links to the others. Read in order, then return to whichever part you need most on a given day.

## Read order

1. [`too-deep.md`](too-deep.md) — **Part 1 of 3 · Outward**  
   Positioning audit of `master_context.md`. The brand, the value you bring, who it lands with, and what to fix in your résumé material.

2. [`fatalism.md`](fatalism.md) — **Part 2 of 3 · Inward**  
   How your thinking model works, why you do what you do, where you fail, and daily/weekly exercises to get stronger.

3. [`too-greedy.md`](too-greedy.md) — **Part 3 of 3 · Forward**  
   The 12-month commitment plan: one domain, one identity, one number, quarter-by-quarter, with guardrails against your own operating system.
