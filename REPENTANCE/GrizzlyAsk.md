# Pyramid Pitch — Bob Lavania

## Practical Value/IP
**What IP could you be paid for?**

I bridge the gap between business strategy and technical execution. My IP is the ability to walk into a chaotic, manual business process, identify the true operational bottleneck that is costing time and money, and build a technological system that fixes it permanently. I don't just manage projects; I diagnose root causes and build the actual software systems to resolve them.

---

## Direction
**What is in demand?**

Every traditional business is being told they need to adopt "AI" or undergo "Digital Transformation" to survive. As a result, they buy or build expensive software that their frontline teams refuse to use. There is massive demand for leaders who can separate technology hype from operational reality and deliver systems that operators trust, adopt, and use to protect profit margins.

---

## Purpose
**What SHOULD you do?**

I should be acting as the connective tissue between the executive boardroom and the technical execution team. My purpose is to stop businesses from wasting capital solving the wrong problems. I should be deployed against high-friction operational challenges—like quoting, dispatch, or intake—to build systems that drive measurable efficiency and scale without adding headcount.

---

## Identity
**Who ARE you? (North Star, WOW Factors)**

I'm a **Bridge**—a pragmatic technical operator who connects business strategy with hands-on technological execution. I bring a builder's mindset and deep operational empathy to complex workflows.
*   **North Star:** The Bridge / The Operational Fixer
*   **WOW 1 (Diagnostic):** I find the root cause, not just the symptom.
*   **WOW 2 (Bilingual):** I speak the language of profitability to executives, and the language of code to engineers. 
*   **WOW 3 (The Builder):** I don't just hand over a strategy deck; I prototype and build the first version of the system myself.
