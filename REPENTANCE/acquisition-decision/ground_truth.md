## Relapse risks (acquisition-specific)

From `fatalism.md` + `too-greedy.md`:

1. **Novelty over compounding** — acquisition research feels like insight; avoids boring distribution reps.
2. **New domain escape** — any non-ops-AI/logistics acquisition violates 12-month anti-reframe rule.
3. **Indefinite lane repeat** — BVXpress was 9 years finance/M&A distribution; left to escape pigeonhole. Acquisition without exit criteria risks the same cage.
4. **Build over sell** — diligence memos and architecture teardowns vs. outbound/job search reps.
5. **Month-5 "more interesting problem"** — explicit failure mode in `too-greedy.md`.

**Acquisition smell test:** If listing reviews replace Pipeline outbound or job-search conversations, it's avoidance theater.
