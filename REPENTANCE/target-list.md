# Ops-AI Target List — Series-A/B Lane

Primary motion: full-time **Founding / Principal Product** at AI-native logistics, freight, dispatch, field-service, and supply-chain ops companies.

**Filter (all must pass):** Series-A to early Series-B · AI-native core product · operational workflow complexity · 5+ engineers · founded ~2019–2024 · North America + remote-tolerant.

**Outreach rule:** Series-A → message founder. Series-B → message Head of Product or VP Engineering. Lead with bottleneck hypothesis + one Quotely proof (60 min→3 min costing, ~50% support deflection, ~93% fill rate). No resume in first message.

**Status key:** `sourced` | `researching` | `contacted` | `conversation` | `applied` | `interview` | `onsite` | `offer` | `rejected` | `ghosted`
