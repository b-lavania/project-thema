# 90-Day Scoreboard & GTM Plan — Ops-AI Role Landing

This is the unified strategic and operational blueprint for landing a full-time **Founding / Principal Product** role at Series-A/B AI-native logistics / ops companies. It combines the 30-day reset, 90-day execution cycle, and 12-month compounding thesis with daily/weekly execution metrics.

---

## Strategic Foundations

### Market Lane & Buyers
*   **Target:** Seed to Series-B AI-native logistics, supply chain, dispatch-heavy, and operational workflow companies where quoting, scheduling, crew allocation, pricing logic, support automation, or AI-enabled reliability are strategic bottlenecks.
*   **Ideal Buyers:** Founders/CEOs, COOs/GMs, Head of Product, VP Ops, or technical founders needing their first product operator.
*   **Positioning Sentence:** *I find the real operational bottleneck, reduce ambiguity, and build the system that makes the fix permanent.*

### Three Proof Pillars
1.  **Reframe:** Speed-to-quote, operational trust, decision architecture, export speed.
2.  **Build:** CV, OR, analytics, workflow systems, AI-assisted operational tooling.
3.  **Commercialize:** 20x faster quoting, 3x take rate, ARPU expansion, retention/workflow adoption gains.

### Portfolio Guidance
*   **`PORTFOLIO/concept1.html` (Primary Spine):** Founding operator, messy-stage builder, technical-commercial translator.
*   **`PORTFOLIO/concept2.html` (Tone & Voice):** Structural intelligence, decision latency, problem framing. Use for messaging.
*   **`PORTFOLIO/CONCEPT3/index.html` (Demoted):** Diluted corporate identity; do not maintain or lead with.

## Outreach Templates & Rules

### LinkedIn Cold (Series-A Founder)
> I built a CV + OR quoting system for last-mile logistics at Quotely — reduced job costing from 60 minutes to 3. I think [Company]'s real bottleneck is [one specific hypothesis from target-list]. Worth a 15-minute conversation?

**Outbound Rules:**
*   No resume attachment in the first message.
*   Lead with their bottleneck, not your biography.
*   One Quotely proof metric max.
*   Outreach in batches of 15; personalize each.

### Warm Intro Ask
> I'm targeting Series-A ops-AI companies in freight/dispatch. Would you be open to intro'ing me to [Name] at [Company]? Context: I ship production AI for quoting and dispatch at Quotely (60min→3min costing, 50% support deflection).

## Weekly Review Questions
Run every Friday in Pipeline → Weekly Review:
1.  Did I move the pipeline, or did I just think about it?
2.  Did I distribute externally and internally, or did I hide in building?
3.  Did every active task connect to interview generation, proof quality, or the one number?
4.  Did I try to escape into a new identity, domain, or elegant side path?

## Psychological Guardrails & Drills

To counter weaknesses (identity fragmentation, novelty addiction, distribution avoidance):

*   **Boring-Rep Streak:** 1 unglamorous action every weekday (e.g., 5 outbound messages or 1 tailored company note).
*   **Finish-Before-Start Rule:** No new asset/project until the current one reaches a written definition of done.
*   **Parking-Lot Rule:** Every exciting new identity, side project, or domain idea goes into the parking lot text area in the CRM—not onto the active calendar.
*   **WIP Limit (1-2):** If more than two active projects/sprints are open, something gets cut.
*   **Evidence Ledger:** Maintain facts for key claims once; stop arguing with the internal skeptic in public.
*   **12-Month Commitments:** Stay locked on **one domain** (logistics/ops-AI), **one identity** (Founding PM), and **one number** (Quotely ARR/metrics). Boredom is a compounding phase, not a signal to pivot.
